ABOUT US

SEJAMO provides quality training courses to equip candidates with the knowledge and skills to meet the work environment needs. A candidate who go through our training will receive both integrated theoretical and practical experience and exposure to the chosen qualification or skills programme. We strive to make life changing contributions towards the well-being, uplifting and the developments of candidates whom we engage with. SEJAMO maintain a strong foundation of trust and mutual respect generated through positive relationships with client. We focus on the needs of our clients whilst ensuring that all provided service and support are in compliance with requirements specified.


WHO WE ARE

SEJAMO was registered and established in 2008 with the aim of providing short duration and high intensity management and skill courses in Construction to companies in South Africa. Over period of years SEJAMO expanded to include qualifications with local government SETA.

SUCCESS

Our success depends on the achievement of each individual in our company. We will provide challenges and opportunities, wherever possible for our employees in order to foster positive personal and technical growth.

FOCUS

SEJAMO recognizes that in order to maintain success in business it is imperative to aim for service excellence and client satisfaction.

MISSION

To provide quality service and to complete projects in the specified time.

VISION

To be leaders in skills development fraternity.
