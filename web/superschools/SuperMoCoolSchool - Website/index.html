SuperSchool offers a fun and easy-to-use Collaborative
Learning Tech Platform that includes:
  • Learning modules that track and support existing curricula

  • Mission-based learning activities that enhance learners'
    leadership and team effectiveness skills

  • Gaming Modules that make STEM (Science Technology
    Engineering and Maths) learning fun. We also include art, design,
    craft and healthy physical activities in the
    curriculum

  • The active exchange of knowledge & interaction accross
    schools, regions and countries.

SUPERSCHOOL addresses South Africa's urgent need to
improve science, technology, engineering, and math(STEM)
skills.

If you are an educator, parent or potential funder: Please
contact us for more details
  • SuperSchool is a Holiday Camp and After-School Space
    - mobile, structured, and scalable.

  • Driven off a digital platform: All participants on the Learning
    Platform become members of the SuperSchool STEM
    Learning Community #MoCool

  • Selected SuperSchool Programmes can be intergrated into
    a school's existing curriculum and STEM activities via the
    Collaborative Platform

  • An artificial intelligence systems approach enables us to
    adapt the Programme to particular school's requirements - and
    individualised learning

  • Our Makerspace, individual and Group Projects, and our
    ongoing Competitions, Hackathons and Challenges keep the
    curriculum relevant and "happening".
