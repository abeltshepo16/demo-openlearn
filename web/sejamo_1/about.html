TRAINING

At SEJAMO we take great pride in the training services that we provide Our vision is to be the industry leader in construction training area. We provide effective programmes to assist construction supervisors and managers to create a productive and harmonious working environment. The training aims at adding value to our clients businesses through pro-active empowerment of staff.

Our Managers/Supervisors training mainly focus on labour/production issues which arise on daily basis. A competent Manager/Supervisors should be able to eliminate common pitfalls which cause problems on a day to day running of a business.

Training is conducted in a professional manner utilizing only our SETA registered Facilitator, Assessors and comprehensive learner guides which are given to each attendee, and can later be used as reference guide. Role plays, discussion forums and exercises form part of the training session which allow for better understanding of the learning materia covered.

We offer all our training programmes at the client's premises or any other agreed venue. The training can be provided on a natinal basis and usually presented when 15 or more candidates require the same training.
